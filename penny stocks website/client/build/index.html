Welcome to my Penny Stocks Classifier

About

Welcome to my Penny Stocks Classifier Website.

This project was started to help retail investors save time who use reddit a source of information but often waste valuable time by looking through a sea of memes and useless, lazy posts in order to uncover gems within the forum. I decided that stock research on reddit should not be such a time consuming process.

I filtered the valuable posts by leveraging machine learning (supervised classification based on stock growths and text data) in order to help any busy user quickly filter out what they want to read.

I used r/pennystocks as the focus of this project because I think investors often illogically overlook these stocks due to the stigma that surrounds them and their reputation for being risky. However, many people tend to forget that many blue-chip companies we think of today were at one point classed as penny stocks.

Some notable examples: Apple in the early 2000s, Ford after both the financial crises and the 2020 stock market crash, Monster Beverage Corp until the late 2000s, Plug Power until 2020, and many more.
